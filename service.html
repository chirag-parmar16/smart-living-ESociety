Services

Kitchen Services

Our centralized kitchen is designed to serve residents with freshly cooked, nutritious meals every day. Whether you prefer vegetarian or non-vegetarian food, our chefs ensure hygienic preparation and timely delivery right to your doorstep. The menu is updated regularly to provide variety, and residents can place orders via the society web portal easily.

  • Fresh meals delivered daily
  • Multiple menu options
  • 100% hygienic preparation
  • Easy ordering via web portal

Housekeeping Services

We provide professional housekeeping to maintain cleanliness and hygiene in your home. Services include daily sweeping, mopping, bathroom cleaning, and trash disposal. Our trained staff follow strict protocols and use eco-friendly products to ensure a safe and clean environment for you and your family. Customizable schedules are available for your convenience.

  • Daily/weekly cleaning options
  • Trained and verified staff
  • Eco-friendly cleaning products
  • Flexible timing as per your need

Grocery Services

With our society-integrated grocery service, residents can shop for all their daily essentials online without stepping out. From fresh vegetables and fruits to packaged foods, dairy, and cleaning products, everything is available through our platform. Orders are delivered promptly, and special discounts are offered on select items for society members.

  • Wide range of daily products
  • Fast and contactless delivery
  • Exclusive member discounts
  • Easy online ordering

Maintenance

Our maintenance team is available to address all technical and household repair needs within the society. From plumbing leaks and electrical issues to appliance servicing and minor carpentry work, you can raise a service request anytime. Our technicians are certified, responsive, and committed to solving problems efficiently to ensure your comfort.

  • Plumbing, electrical & AC repairs
  • Certified technicians
  • Quick response time
  • Raise requests via dashboard
